Sankalp Theatre Event: Short movie Screening



A short film is any motion picture not long enough to be considered a feature film

  • THE TIME DURATION OF THE SHORT MOVIE MUST BE 12+1 MINS(INCLUDING CREDITS)

  • ALL THE PARTICIPANTS IN THE SHORT MOVIE MUST BE FROM THE SAME COLLEGE

  • THE SHORT MOVIE WILL BE JUDGED BASED ON THE CREATIVITY EXHIBITED IN THE SUBJECT CHOSEN,SKILLS,ACTORS SCREEN PRESCENCE,AUDIO DUBBING QUALITY,POST PRODUCTION QUALITY

  • ATHE MOVIE MUST NOT TAKE ANY REFERENCES OF THE COLLEGE OR THE FACULTY IN AN OFFENSIVE MANNER

  • THE MOVIE MUST NOT TAKE ANY REFERENCES OF THE COLLEGE OR THE FACULTY IN AN OFFENSIVE MANNER

  • THE PARTICIPANTS MUST NOT MAKE USE OF ANY VULGAR DIALOGUES OR VULGAR IMITATIONS

  • THE POST PRODUCTION JOB MUST BE DONE BY THE STUDENTS ONLY. IF IDENTIFIED THAT IT HAS BE DONE OUTSIDE, THE TEAM WILL BE DIRECTLY DISQUALIFIED

  • LAST DATE TO SUBMIT YOUR SHORT MOVIES IS ON OR BEFORE 5 MARCH(MONDAY)

  • SHORT MOVIES HAVE TO BE SUBMITTED IN A PENDRIVE WHICH INTURN MUST BE ENCLOSED IN A ENVELOPE.(WRITE THE NAME OF THE SHORT MOVIE,BRANCH,SEMESTER AND CONTACT NUMBER ON THE ENVELOPE)
