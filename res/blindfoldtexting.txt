BLINDFOLD TEXTING

DESCRIPTION:-
	This event is all about the skills you have in typing a text dictated in basic keypad phone.



RULES:-
1.	There will be one preliminary round and further more rounds will be based on the number of participants.

2.	Participants will be given a basic mobile phone and will be blindfolded and they have to type whatever is dictated         to them.

3.	Speed and Accuracy are the main factors considered to select the participant to the further rounds.

4.	The difficulty is increased by increasing the speed of dictation and the usage of special symbols.

#There is no entry fee for this event.
#There will an attractive cash prize for the winner and the runner-up.
