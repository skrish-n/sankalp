Sankalp Technical Flagship Event: Blindfold Texting



This event is all about the skills you have in typing a text dictated in basic keypad phone

  • Blindfold Texting is an individual event, the winner stands alone

  • There will be one preliminary round and further more rounds will be based on the number of participants

  • Participants will be given a basic mobile phone and will be blindfolded and they have to type whatever is dictated to them

  • Speed and Accuracy are the main factors considered to select the participant to the further rounds

  • The difficulty is increased by increasing the speed of dictation and the usage of special symbols

  • Organizer's decisions are final
